Our Process

1. SEO Audit

Our SEO audits use sophisticated technology to analyze how search engines view your site, identify problems and structure content for maximum conversion within the context of constant change.

2. SEO Analysis

Our SEO analysis includes competitive keyword research, technical analysis, on-page and off-page analysis. We will also compare you to your competitors so that we can implement the right strategy for your business.

3. SEO Strategy

Our SEO strategy will come from our analysis of your current website and competitors. We will look for low-hanging fruit, while focusing on the long-term SEO strategy that gets you the results you are looking for.

4. SEO Execute

Our SEO execution will start with fixing any technical website issues and on-page optimization that is needed. From there we will begin to focus on your off-page SEO such as link building for relevant content on relevant websites.

5. SEO Report

We will provide you with monthly ongoing SEO reporting to analyze your SEO movement. This includes your ongoing keyword performance.

6. SEO Optimization

Ongoing SEO optimization will be needed for things like blogs, articles, content creation, etc... In addition, there are constant search engine rule changes from Google that will need to be paid attention to.
